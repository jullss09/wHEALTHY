Mental health

Mental health is very important at every stage of our life as it affects how we think, feel, and act. It determines how we handle stress, relate to others, and make life choices.

HOW TO TAKE CARE OF YOUR MENTAL HEALTH

  1. Talking about your feelings will help you stay in good mental health especially at times of distress.
  2. Improve your Physical Health by exercising. This will help you concentrate and sleep better.
  3. Always take a break and reward yourself.A few minutes can be enough to de-stress!
  4. Ask for help when needed. After all we are all humans, we sometimes get overwhelmed by how we feel. Talk to family or friends if it feels to much.
  5. Accept who you are. We are all different and unique; feel good about yourself!
  6. Meditate and reflect.
